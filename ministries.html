Ministries in the church

Worship Ministry

This ministry is responsible for leading congregational worship, which may include music, singing, and other forms of worship.

Children's Ministry

This ministry is focused on providing age-appropriate teaching and activities for children, often through Sunday School programs, Vacation Bible School, and other events.

Youth Ministry

This ministry is geared toward teenagers and young adults, and may include Bible studies, social events, retreats, and other activities.

Outreach Ministry

This ministry is focused on reaching out to the community and sharing the message of the gospel with those who may not attend church.

Small Group Ministry

This ministry involves forming smaller groups within the larger congregation for the purpose of fellowship, Bible study, and accountability.

Missions Ministry

This ministry is focused on supporting missionaries and mission work around the world, often through fundraising, prayer, and other forms of support.

Prayer Ministry

This ministry involves leading and coordinating prayer efforts within the church, both individually and corporately.

Counseling Ministry

This ministry may provide counseling and support to individuals and families within the church who are dealing with various challenges and struggles.
